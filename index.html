Designs That Tell Stories

Mehndi, also known as henna, is an ancient art form that transforms hands and feet into intricate canvases of tradition and beauty. From delicate floral motifs to bold geometric patterns, each design carries cultural significance, symbolizing joy, celebration, and blessings. Whether for weddings, festivals, or special occasions, mehndi designs are more than just decoration‚Äîthey are timeless stories drawn in shades of tradition. Popular styles include Arabic mehndi, Indian bridal mehndi, and contemporary fusion designs, each blending creativity with heritage.
