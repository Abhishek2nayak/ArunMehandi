Who are we ?

At Arun Mehandi art , we specialize in professional Mehndi and henna design services for every special occasion—Bridal Mehndi, Arabic Mehndi, Baby Showers, Engagements, Eid, and more. With years of experience and a passion for creativity, our skilled artists deliver intricate, long-lasting designs that reflect both tradition and style.
We’re proud to be a trusted name in custom henna artistry, known for making every client feel confident and celebrated. Whether it’s your wedding day or a joyful gathering, we’re here to make your moment memorable with our unique, handcrafted Mehndi designs.
