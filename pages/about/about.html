About Arun Mehandi Art

Celebrating Tradition with Creativity Since 2015

At Arun Mehandi Art, we specialize in professional Mehndi and henna designs for every special occasion — Bridal Mehndi, Arabic Mehndi, Baby Showers, Engagements, Eid, and more. Our skilled artists bring years of experience and a deep passion for creativity, ensuring every design is intricate, long-lasting, and tailored to your unique style.
Mehndi is more than just decoration — it’s a tradition, a blessing, and a form of self-expression. That’s why we work closely with our clients to create personalized designs that reflect their personality, cultural heritage, and the joy of their celebration.
From delicate patterns for intimate gatherings to elaborate bridal masterpieces, we pride ourselves on being a trusted name in henna artistry. Whether it’s your wedding day or a festive celebration, we make your moment truly unforgettable with our handcrafted designs.
