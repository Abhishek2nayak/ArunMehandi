About Chintu | The Henna Vibe

Chintu | The Henna Vibe is the best mehndi artist in Phagwara, Punjab, bringing together creativity, culture, and natural beauty. With over 12+ years of experience and a team of 15+ professional mehndi artists, we craft stunning bridal, festive, and custom henna designs using 100% organic mehndi.

About The Henna Vibe

Phagwara’s Most Trusted Mehndi Artist Team

Founded by Chintu, The Henna Vibe has become a leading name in bridal mehndi services in Punjab. From traditional Indian bridal designs to Arabic, Mandala, and Baby Shower Mehndi, every stroke reflects elegance, precision, and artistry. Our team only uses natural, chemical-free henna to ensure safe application and rich, deep color.
We offer home mehndi services across Phagwara and nearby areas for engagements, Karwa Chauth, Eid, Diwali, and other festive occasions. Our designs are a blend of tradition and innovation — making every client’s experience comfortable, memorable, and stylish.
Whether you're looking for modern fusion mehndi or intricate bridal patterns, our experienced artists bring your imagination to life with passion and professionalism.
